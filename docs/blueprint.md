# **App Name**: Sivam Market Online

## Core Features:

- Home Page: Display a hero section with supermarket information, key features, customer ratings, and prominent call-to-action buttons.
- About Us Page: Showcase the supermarket's story, highlighting its reliability, commitment to the community, and services that address local needs.
- Products Page: Organize and display product categories like groceries, vegetables & fruits, household essentials, snacks & beverages, and personal care.
- Services Page: Describe available services such as in-store shopping, door delivery (if available), and customer assistance.
- Reviews Page: Display customer reviews from Google, highlighting positive feedback and transparently addressing critical comments.
- Contact Page: Provide contact information, store address, a map placeholder, and a contact form for customer inquiries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm green (#60A9A6) to evoke freshness and a connection to healthy, natural foods.
- Background color: Off-white (#F2F0EB), a gentle desaturation of the primary hue, to provide a clean and inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Orange (#A96063), an analogous color to green, to create contrast and draw attention to calls to action and important information.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif offering both modernity and approachability, making it perfect for readability across the site.
- Use clear, intuitive icons for product categories and services, enhancing the user experience with visual cues.
- Implement a mobile-first responsive design, ensuring a seamless shopping experience on all devices with a sticky top navigation bar and a well-organized footer.
- Incorporate subtle animations like a testimonial slider and animated hero section to engage users and enhance their browsing experience.
